Hokie-owned Business Directory

Hokies supporting Hokies!

In an effort to celebrate Hokies with an entrepreneurial spirit during these uncertain times, LAUNCH: the Center for New Ventures at Virginia Tech, in partnership with the Apex Center for Entrepreneurs, has established an opt-in directory of Hokie-owned businesses. These ventures are founded or operated by alumni, faculty or staff. Submit your company listing or connect with other Hokie-owned businesses today!

This directory is intended to help the Hokie community to connect and support each other, build our entrepreneurial ecosystem and recognize entrepreneurial Hokies everywhere!

Submit an Hokie-Owned Business

Are you a Hokie who owns a business? Submit the form below to request your enterprise be included in the directory. Submissions will be reviewed for completeness and updated on a weekly basis. The directory is populated only by submissions we have received and is provided for informational purposes only. We hope you explore new connections and businesses that expand your own Hokie community!


If you need to make a correction or delete your listing, please email aluminate@vt.edu.
