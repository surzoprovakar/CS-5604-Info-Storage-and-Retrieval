Advertising

The advertising major prepares students to promote products, services, ideas and personalities through paid media. The major combines coursework from communication, business, and visual design. Popular topics include consumer behavior, advertising account planning, media buying, brand storytelling, copy design and production, and campaign evaluation. Youll have class projects that design advertising strategies and materials for real clients, find internships that put skills learned in the classroom into practice, and interact with advertising-industry practitioners.

Career Options

  • Advertising account executive
  • Creative director
  • Media buyer
  • Outdoor advertising designer
  • Copywriter
  • Digital advertising planner
