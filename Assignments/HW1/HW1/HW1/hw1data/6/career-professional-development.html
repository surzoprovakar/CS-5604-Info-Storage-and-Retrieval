Career and Professional Development

Empowering students to pursue a path to a fulfilling career requires more than simply helping them earn a degree.

At Virginia Tech, our Career and Professional Development advisors help Hokies explore potential careers and majors, land internships, and tailor résumés for future employment. Whether you need a 10-minute walk-in meeting or a scheduled appointment, we are available and ready to help you.

No matter your major or academic level, we aim to help students explore career options, seek real-world experiences, secure post-graduation employment, and plan for graduate or professional school. In fact, by the time graduation rolls around, 93 percent of our grads have had a career-related experience while at Virginia Tech, such as paid internships, part-time and full-time jobs, or research experience.

We’re here to serve you and start you on your path, from success at Virginia Tech to far beyond.
