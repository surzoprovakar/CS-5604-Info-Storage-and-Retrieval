    Daily Admissions Campus Visits

    Thank you for your interest in visiting Virginia Tech! Click on the date below that works best for your schedule to see available sessions.

    Visit Format:
    Our visit includes a 30-minute information session and a 60-minute campus walking tour. 

    Mask Expectations:
    Beginning on August 10, 2021, Virginia Tech will require masks for indoor public spaces regardless of vaccination status. As of now, there is no requirement for masks in outdoor settings. This aligns Virginia Tech with the recently updated guidance from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). For additional information on this requirement, please see this website.
     
    If you do not have a mask when you visit, one will be provided to you to join our information session that will be taking place in an indoor, public space. We will communicate any updates to this policy by email.

    More information concerning summer and fall COVID-19 plans are available here.

    Note: Undergraduate Admissions continues to monitor the COVID-19 pandemic. As public health guidelines change, programming is subject to change as needed.
