CS4634: Design of Information

Survey of the higher-order properties that allow data to become information, that is, to inform people.  The course focuses on the analysis of user needs, user comprehension and local semantics; the design of information organization; and the design of information display appropriate to use and setting. (3H, 3C)

Prerequisites: a grade of C or better in CS 3114 and CS 3724.

See: course web pages.

Taught By: Doug Bowman
