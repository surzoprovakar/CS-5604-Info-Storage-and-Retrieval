    CS|Source Stakeholder Spotlight: CoStar Group , article

    CoStar Group is passionate about connecting with the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech. It strives to build partnerships with a variety of student groups, including Association for Women in Computing, Cyber Club, and VT Hacks. They also partner with professors and advisors to ensure they are being a strong partner to the Virginia Tech computer science students.
