university of illinois is a public research university, ultimately responsible to the citizens of illinois and the illinois general assembly. authority over the university is held by a board of trustees. a president is the chief executive officer for the university system, and each of three campuses is headed by a chancellor, vice chancellors who have campuswide responsibilities, and other administrators whose duties are delegated by the chancellor. chancellor, university of illinois at urbana-champaign vice president, university of illinois the chancellor is the chief executive officer of the campus. the office of the chancellor has the all-encompassing goal of ensuring the overall excellence of the urbana-champaign campus. vice chancellors, associate chancellors, and directors report directly to the chancellor. about robert jones vice chancellor for academic affairs and provost the provost is the chief academic and budget officer for the campus. the office oversees academic and budgetary policy and priorities, ensures the quality of the faculty and student body, and maintains educational excellence. all deans and directors of academic units report to the provost. about
