tech, and you’re counting the days till you come to blacksburg. we get it: now you can jumpstart your first year with summer academy! summer academy gives incoming freshmen and transfer students a head start on campus life before the busyness of the fall semester. for six weeks, eat in the dining halls, live in the residence halls, and earn six or more credits in much smaller class sizes — all while you’re making friends to last a lifetime. learn more about summer academy summer session soak up the beauty of blacksburg in the summer: attend summer session at virginia tech, with two short ahead? winter session is condensed into a three-week period over winter break. if you’re double majoring, planning to graduate early, or simply hoping to lighten your course load for fall or spring,
