provides a broad background in scientific inquiry, quantitative problem solving, emerging research frontiers, and communication skills. students can pursue either a b.s. or a b.a. according to their career goals. the b.s. is recommended for students interested in graduate study or in leadership or technical positions in industry or government. it may lead to a 5-year b.s./m.s. for qualified
