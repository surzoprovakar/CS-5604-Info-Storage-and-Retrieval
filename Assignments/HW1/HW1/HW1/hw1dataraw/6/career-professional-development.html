ready1 career and professional development empowering students to pursue a path to a fulfilling career requires more than simply helping them earn a degree. at virginia tech, our career and professional development advisors help hokies explore potential careers and majors, land internships, and tailor résumés for future employment. whether you need a 10-minute walk-in meeting or a scheduled appointment, we are available and ready to help you. no matter your major or academic level, we aim to help students explore career options, seek real-world experiences, secure post-graduation employment, and plan for graduate or professional school. in fact, by the time graduation rolls around, 93 percent of our grads have had a career-related experience while at virginia tech, such as paid internships, part-time and full-time jobs, or research experience. we’re here to serve you and start you on your path, from success at virginia tech to far beyond. learn more about
